What is Dokkie?

With Dokkie, planning trips becomes a lot easier! Invite your friends to a fun activity, divide the costs easy and keep track of everything clearly. No more confusion about who paid what or how much you owe. Dokkie gives you a clear overview of all payments, so you can fully focus on enjoying your trip. No fuss, just have fun together!
